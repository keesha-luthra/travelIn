Itinerary



  • Delhi - Haridwar (1 Night)

    Tour guests will arrive at Delhi Airport as per their scheduled flight. Upon arrival they will meet the TravelIN tour manager at the airport and proceed to Haridwar, considered one of the seven holiest places in India. In the evening we experience the mesmerising Ganga Aarti at Har Ki Pauri followed by a short meet and greet session.


  • Haridwar - Rishikesh - Mussoorie (2 Nights)

    Today we visit Prati Badri Kedarnath Temple, Lakshman Mandir, and Lakshman Jhula, a famous hanging bridge across the river Ganges. Later, we take a thrilling white-water rafting experience at Rishikesh. Further we proceed to Mussoorie, also known as Queen of the Hills.


  • Mussoorie

    Today we see the beautiful Kempty Falls, which offers a mesmerizing panoramic view as the water falls from the mountains. Later we enjoy Ropeway Ride at Gun Hill point- a favourite place for viewing picturesque Himalayan range. At Gun Hill point you can photograph yourself in a Pahadi outfit at your own cost. In the evening we have some free time for shopping. Note: Please carry a swim-suit while visiting Kempty falls


  • Mussoorie - Nainital (3 Nights)

    Today we proceed to Nainital, commonly known as the Lake District of India. Upon arrival we have free time to relax and enjoy the surroundings.
