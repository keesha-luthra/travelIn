Itinerary

  • Jaipur (2 Nights)

    Tour guests fly to Jaipur and proceed to the city. Later we visit City Palace, Jantar Mantar, and the Sound and Light Show at Amer Fort before a short meet and greet session.

  • Jaipur

    Today we visit Amer Fort, Panna Meena ka Kund, and Hawa Mahal. Later we have free time for shopping at Bapu Market.

  • Jaipur - Pushkar (1 Night)

    Today we proceed to Pushkar. En route we visit Kishangarh, then visit Brahma Mandir, Pushkar Tirth, and the evening aarti at Pushkar Ghat.

  • Pushkar - Chittorgarh - Udaipur (2 Nights)

    Today we visit Chittorgarh Fort, Ranakumbha Palace, Meera Mandir, Vijay Stambha, and Queen Padmini's Palace. Later we proceed to Udaipur.

  • Udaipur

    We visit Moti Magri, explore City Palace, enjoy a boat ride to Jag Mandir on Lake Pichola, and close the evening with a traditional music and dance show.

  • Udaipur - Departure

    Tour guests proceed to Udaipur airport as per their scheduled flight and start their return journey home. Tour concludes.
